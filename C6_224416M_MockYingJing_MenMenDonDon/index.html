About Men Men Don Don

In order for us to deliver our Fast, Affordable, Delicious bowls of rice and noodles, we would like to introduce our preparations and ingredients:


  1.  Chicken meat that has been marinated slowly in our special sauce for more than 48 hours.
  2.  Specially made Char Siew and Stewed Pork that is boiled over for a day.
  3.  Pork used for Tonkatsu are marinated for more than 12 hours in traditional Japanese fermented condiment.
  4.  Salmons are from Faroe Islands in Denmark.
  5.  Scallops are from Aomori prefecture in Japan.
